  • Superjunior_D&E

    Super Junior D&E is a boy band formed under the Super Junior on December 16, 2011, named after Donghae's "D" and Eunhyuk's "E". The group was first introduced during SUPER JUNIOR's third concert tour, SUPER SHOW 3, with the song "I Wanna Love You". After releasing the digital single "Oppa, Oppa" at the end of the same year, D&E began the official activities.
